01. Background

About Me

I am a self-taught AI engineer and researcher based in Afghanistan. Driven by a deep-seated curiosity about how systems acquire, process, and generalize patterns, I chose the path of independent study. To me, intelligence research is the most challenging and meaningful engineering problem of our time.

My philosophy is grounded in first-principles learning. I believe that to truly master complex computational tools, one must understand the underlying layers—from transistor limitations and memory hierarchies to the linear algebra governing backpropagation.

As a solo founder-developer, I design and write systems from scratch. By rejecting reliance on black-box frameworks and instead rebuilding basic building blocks—whether a custom tensor structure, a compiler layout, or a P2P networking handler—I seek to cultivate deep expertise in AI infrastructure and high-performance engineering.
